# Customer Journey Map for Code-Cleanser

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developers encounter complex codebases that are difficult to maintain and understand.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available tools; existing solutions are either too complex or not tailored for specific programming languages.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, overwhelm, confusion.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create engaging content (blogs, videos) showcasing the benefits of using Code-Cleanser; offer free webinars on code refactoring best practices.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content engagement rates (likes, shares, comments).

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developers actively search for solutions to simplify their codebases.
- **Friction Points**: Information overload; difficulty in comparing features and benefits of different tools; skepticism about AI capabilities.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious; anxiety about making the wrong choice.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear comparison charts, testimonials, and case studies; offer a free trial or demo to alleviate concerns.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and trial sign-ups.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developers decide to try Code-Cleanser after initial research.
- **Friction Points**: Installation challenges; initial learning curve; potential bugs or issues during the trial.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension; eagerness to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer comprehensive onboarding support, tutorials, and a responsive customer support team to assist with any issues.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement metrics during the trial period.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developers experience positive results from using Code-Cleanser and decide to integrate it into their workflow.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing tools and processes; potential resistance from team members.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction and relief; pride in improving code quality.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide integration guides, success stories from similar teams, and incentives for team-wide adoption.
- **Metric**: Number of licenses purchased and user retention rate.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developers and teams recognize the value of Code-Cleanser and seek to expand its use across more projects or teams.
- **Friction Points**: Budget constraints; need for additional training or resources for new users.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for expansion; concern about costs and resource allocation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer volume discounts, advanced training sessions, and a community forum for sharing best practices.
- **Metric**: Revenue growth from upsells and expansion licenses; user engagement in community forums.
