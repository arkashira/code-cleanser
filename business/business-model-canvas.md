# Business Model Canvas for Code-Cleanser

## Customer Segments
- **Software Development Teams**: Teams working on large, complex codebases that require regular refactoring.
- **Freelance Developers**: Independent developers seeking tools to enhance productivity and code quality.
- **Tech Startups**: New companies looking to establish clean code practices from the outset.
- **Enterprise Companies**: Large organizations with legacy systems needing modernization and simplification.

## Value Propositions
- **AI-Driven Refactoring**: Automatically simplifies complex code, reducing technical debt and improving maintainability.
- **Increased Productivity**: Saves developers time by streamlining the refactoring process, allowing them to focus on feature development.
- **Enhanced Code Quality**: Improves overall code quality, leading to fewer bugs and easier onboarding for new developers.
- **Cost-Effective Solution**: Reduces the need for extensive manual code reviews and refactoring efforts, saving companies money.

## Channels
- **Direct Sales**: Selling licenses through the company website.
- **Partnerships with Development Platforms**: Integrating with platforms like GitHub and GitLab for seamless access.
- **Online Marketplaces**: Listing on software marketplaces (e.g., AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace).
- **Developer Conferences and Meetups**: Showcasing the product at industry events to attract early adopters.

## Customer Relationships
- **Self-Service Resources**: Comprehensive documentation and tutorials available online for users to learn independently.
- **Community Support**: Building a user community for sharing tips, best practices, and troubleshooting.
- **Dedicated Support**: Offering tiered support plans for enterprise customers, including direct access to technical support teams.
- **Feedback Loops**: Regularly soliciting user feedback to improve the product and address user needs.

## Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Model**: Monthly or annual subscriptions for access to the tool, priced at $29/month (approx. 1,000 THB) for individual users and $299/month (approx. 10,500 THB) for teams.
- **Enterprise Licensing**: Custom pricing for large organizations based on the number of users and specific needs.
- **Consulting Services**: Offering professional services for codebase assessments and tailored refactoring strategies.
- **Freemium Model**: Basic version available for free with premium features available through subscription.

## Key Resources
- **Development Team**: Skilled engineers and AI specialists to build and maintain the product.
- **AI Algorithms**: Proprietary algorithms for code analysis and refactoring.
- **Customer Support Team**: Trained personnel to assist users and handle inquiries.
- **Marketing Team**: Professionals to promote the product and manage customer relationships.

## Key Activities
- **Product Development**: Continuous improvement of the code-cleanser tool based on user feedback and technological advancements.
- **Marketing and Sales**: Engaging in marketing campaigns to raise awareness and drive sales.
- **Customer Support**: Providing assistance and resources to users for optimal product usage.
- **Partnership Development**: Establishing relationships with other tech companies and platforms for integrations.

## Key Partners
- **Development Platforms**: Collaborating with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket for integrations.
- **AI Research Institutions**: Partnering with universities or research labs for access to cutting-edge AI technologies.
- **Tech Communities**: Engaging with developer communities and forums for promotion and feedback.
- **Consulting Firms**: Partnering with firms that can offer the tool as part of their development services.

## Cost Structure
- **Development Costs**: Salaries for engineers and AI specialists, estimated at $500,000 annually (approx. 17,500,000 THB).
- **Marketing Expenses**: Budget for online advertising, events, and promotions, estimated at $100,000 annually (approx. 3,500,000 THB).
- **Customer Support Costs**: Salaries for support staff and resources, estimated at $150,000 annually (approx. 5,250,000 THB).
- **Operational Costs**: Hosting, software licenses, and other overhead, estimated at $50,000 annually (approx. 1,750,000 THB).
